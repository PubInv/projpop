Experimenting with Propulsion

If we want to get to the stars, we are going to have to explore some ideas. This is an attempt to explore the idea of space propulsion by constructing spatially distributed dynamic machines. Another way of putting this is to study propulsion by throwing and catching projectile. A third way of thinking about this is that we are attempting to cheat the Tsiolkovsky equation.

Cheating Tsiolkovsky

The mathematics and academmic description of what we are trying to do here is Cheating Tsiolkovsky.

Simulation of the Dynamic Machines

A simulation of momentum-transfer machines/systems has been written. At present it shows the simple fact that if a rocket used a mass driver to fire a projectile backward and if this perfectly and elastically bounced back to it, it would end up going about 3 time faster than ejecting the same mass as a gas at the same velocity.
